Luc is a young adult with a passion for graphic design, mobile/web development, user interface, and user experience.

His interest in the field started in 2018 when he launched his own business Owl Dev. selling a multitude of graphic designs and coding snippets for enthusiasts of the Roblox universe, a video game platform made to create and play other's creations. Born and raised in France, he spent his youthful years there before relocating to Canada. Living in diverse regions around the world has provided him with a well-rounded perspective on design. He gained valuable insights into various design interpretations worldwide and observed how different cultures approach design uniquely.
